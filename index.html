MyDuka (2023)

Myduka Is a Desktop App for managing inventory and sales in retail shops.

I built this application to help a friend manage their inventory and sales more effectively.

The app was built using Python and Tkinter for the GUI, with a focus on simplicity and ease of use.

I used SQLite for the database to store product information and sales data. This allowed for rapid development and deployment.

  • Sales Tracking
  • Reporting and Analytics
  • Export Inventory and Sales Documentation to Excel Sheets
  • Simple User Interface with Customized Themes
